== 3. Register Trademark in Europe Under the New Association

=== Why

Be able to:

* formalize trademark ownership under the association
* protect the brand and logo across Europe
* license the use of the trademark

=== stepes

. Confirm the final brand name and logo
. Run an availability check and legal clearance search
. Define the appropriate trademark classes
. File the European Union trademark application in the name of the association
. Track the application, respond to any objections, and complete registration
. Store all registration records and renewal dates in the association's files
